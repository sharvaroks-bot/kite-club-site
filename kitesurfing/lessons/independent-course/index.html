A 5-day program to become confident and independent in different conditions.

Book Now Back to Courses

Ride upwind, gybe and ride independently

Phase 1 — Upwind mastery

  • Full Beginner Course included — waterstart and riding confirmed or reviewed day 1
  • Upwind edging drill: heel pressure, back-foot drive and kite position for pointing higher
  • Upwind runs: gaining ground up the beach instead of drifting constantly downwind
  • Kite trim and depower: reducing drag while maintaining board speed

Phase 2 — Turns and transitions

  • Gybe: completing a downwind turn smoothly without crashing or stopping
  • Toeside riding introduction: board edge change mid-run
  • Wind awareness: responding to lulls and gusts in real conditions

Phase 3 — Full independence and certification

  • Self-rescue: pack the kite to leading edge, float back to shore unaided
  • Self-launch: inflate, rig, launch and ride with no beach assistant
  • Self-land: bring the kite down and secure it without help
  • IKO International Kiteboarding Certificate · 10h private / 15h group · 4-5 days
