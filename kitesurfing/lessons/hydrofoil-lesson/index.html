Fly above the water on a hydrofoil board. Private 1-hour session for independent kitesurfers.

Book Now Back to Courses

Take flight above the water

Hydrofoil kitesurfing is the next step after mastering independent riding. You will learn to lift the board out of the water and fly above the surface on a foil — a completely new sensation that changes the way you experience the ocean.

This private 1-hour session is designed for kitesurfers who can already ride upwind independently. Your IKO-certified instructor will guide you through foil setup, water starts, and your first flights above the water.

What you will learn

  • Setup the foil board & adjustments
  • Foot placement on the foil board
  • Controlling the lift
  • Riding above the water on foil
  • Body drag with foil board
  • Water start on foil
  • Riding upwind on foil
  • Change direction on foil
  • Self rescue with foil board

FAQ

You must be able to ride independently upwind on a regular kiteboard before attempting hydrofoil. The foil adds complexity and requires good kite control and board skills.

Most students get their first sustained flights within the 1-hour session. Your instructor will set up the foil at an appropriate mast height to give you the best chance of early success.

Yes. All equipment is included — foil board, hydrofoil, kite, bar, harness, helmet and impact vest. You do not need to bring or own any foiling equipment.

Like all kitesurfing disciplines, hydrofoil has risks. We mitigate these with proper instruction, impact protection, correct foil settings and choosing appropriate conditions. Students with good independent riding skills progress safely.
