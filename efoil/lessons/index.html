Your Experience

How Your
Session Works

No wind needed. No experience required. E-foil sessions are available every day — just you, a silent electric board and an instructor beside you in the water.

Every session starts with a 15-minute land briefing covering the wireless remote, safety leash and what to expect. Then we enter the shallow lagoon at Thong Sala where your instructor guides you step by step.

From lying on the board to standing, to your first foil lift above the water — most students reach full foiling within a single 60-90 minute session. GoPro video is included in every booking.

What Happens
In Each Session

Phase 1

Land Briefing

  • How the wireless remote works
  • Speed control — squeeze to go, release to stop
  • How the safety leash works
  • How to fall correctly and safely
  • What the foil does and how it lifts
  • What to expect in the water
Phase 2

Water Progression

  • Lying flat on the board — get comfortable
  • Kneeling position — feel the board move
  • Standing up — instructor holds board steady
  • First solo glides at low speed
  • Feeling the foil start to lift under you
  • First foil lifts above the water!
Phase 3

Flying & Control

  • Consistent foiling runs
  • Speed management with the remote
  • Turning left and right while foiling
  • Recovering from touchdowns gracefully
  • Longer independent runs
  • Carving turns (Freedom Session)
