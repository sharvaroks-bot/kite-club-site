March Questions

Yes — March is peak season. The SE trade winds blow at 15–22 knots, the lagoon is flat, and most days are rideable. February is a close second. April is equally good. Beginners progress faster in March than any other month because lessons almost never get rescheduled.

Book at least 2–3 weeks in advance for March. It is the busiest month of the year at Kite Club — accommodation on the island also fills up quickly. Message us on WhatsApp to check availability: +66 96 720 3910.

At 20–22 knots: 11–12m kite. At 22–26 knots: 9–10m. At 26+ knots: 7–9m. Beginners use school kites chosen by the instructor — typically 12–14m to ensure controlled power and safe learning.

Yes — March has 28–32°C air temperature and 28°C water. There is very little rain. It is one of the driest months on the island. Ideal beach weather between sessions.
