Koh Phangan Wind & Weather Guide

Two Kite Seasons

SE Trade Winds (February–April) — the main kitesurfing season. Consistent 12–25 kn from the SE with flat water at Thong Sala. March and April average 20+ rideable days. Best for beginners.

SW Monsoon (June–September) — variable 10–20 kn from the SW. Works at Thong Sala and Baan Tai. More challenging, suitable for intermediate riders.

Gulf of Thailand Tides

Koh Phangan has a diurnal tide — one high and one low per day. Typical range 0.4–2.2 m. The chart uses a sinusoidal model with the ~50-minute daily phase shift characteristic of the Gulf of Thailand.

Best kitesurfing: wind 15–25 kn from SE, wave height below 0.5 m, incoming tide. Check live wind above before booking via WhatsApp.
