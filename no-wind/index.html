Rescheduling Policy

Wind Too Light?
Free Reschedule.

If wind conditions are not suitable for kite, wing foil or windsurf lessons, we reschedule your session at no extra charge. We monitor the forecast daily and message you by 8pm the evening before your lesson.

✓ Jan–Apr (SE Season)
Most days have usable wind. Rescheduling rare — typically 1–2 light-wind days per month.
✓ May–Sep (SW Season)
Consistent wind, occasional gusty days. Some lessons may be rescheduled.
May / Oct–Nov (Off Season)
Light variable winds. Wind lessons less reliable — e-foil, kayak, SUP always available.

No-Wind Questions

We reschedule the lesson at no extra charge. You will not lose a session day — it simply moves to the next suitable day. During peak SE season (Jan–Apr), low-wind days average about 1–2 per month. We contact you by 8pm the evening before if conditions are not suitable.

Yes — e-foil sessions run year-round, every day, regardless of wind speed. The electric motor means you don't need any wind at all. Sessions from 2,000 THB. Book via WhatsApp the same morning if you like.

E-foil is the easiest — instructors walk alongside you in shallow water and you can fly on your first attempt within 30 minutes. Kayaking is also very beginner-friendly — no skill required, just paddle and enjoy the coastline. SUP rental is a good option if you want to go at your own pace.
