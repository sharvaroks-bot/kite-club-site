The Sport

Stand Up
Paddleboarding

SUP (Stand Up Paddleboarding) is one of the most accessible water activities in the world. You stand on a large, buoyant board and use a long paddle to propel yourself across the water. Simple, peaceful, and a surprisingly effective full-body workout — core, balance, shoulders and legs all engaged.

On Koh Phangan's flat lagoon, SUP is perfect for complete beginners. The calm, protected waters mean you can focus entirely on balance and paddle technique without worrying about waves or currents. Most people are standing confidently within 10 minutes.

SUP is also excellent cross-training for kitesurfers and wing foilers wanting to develop water confidence and core strength on rest days. Or simply enjoy a quiet morning paddle as the sun rises over the Gulf of Thailand.

What to
Experience

🌅

Sunrise Paddle

The most peaceful time to be on the water. Glass-flat lagoon, golden light across the Gulf of Thailand, and almost no one else around. Rent early and have the ocean to yourself.

💪

Full Body Workout

SUP engages your core, shoulders, legs and balance simultaneously. A 2-hour session burns as many calories as a gym workout — but with a much better view. Popular with kitesurfers on rest days.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Activity

SUP boards are wide and stable — perfect for families. Kids can sit or kneel on the nose while a parent paddles. The flat, calm lagoon makes it safe and fun for all ages from 5 upwards.

🧘

SUP Yoga

The ultimate balance challenge. Try your yoga poses on the water — the natural instability of the board turns every posture into a core workout. Ask us about SUP yoga sessions on the lagoon.

SUP Rental Koh Phangan: Guide to Stand-Up Paddleboarding

SUP Rental Koh Phangan: Paddling in Paradise

There is the Koh Phangan of postcards — bustling beaches, vibrant nightlife. Then there is the one you discover yourself. A quieter island that reveals itself in the gentle rhythm of a paddle slicing through the clear sea. This is the world that stand-up paddleboarding unlocks. Not just a sport — a silent conversation with the water, an adventure that moves at the speed of your own curiosity.

Why Koh Phangan is Perfect for SUP

The island's coastline is a masterpiece of sheltered bays and natural harbours that protect the water from the open ocean. The result: vast expanses of warm, shallow water. This is the ultimate environment for learning stand-up paddleboarding. The first wobbly attempt to stand happens in a wonderfully forgiving setting — a fall isn't a failure, it's just a refreshing dip. Calm sea conditions let newcomers focus on the fun, not on fighting the elements.

Finding Your Board

A quality SUP rental spot provides more than just equipment. They help you choose the right board for your skill level — from a wide, stable platform for beginners to something more agile for experienced paddleboarders. Inflatable SUP boards are a surprisingly sturdy and convenient option, and full sets including paddle and leash are always included.

Adventures for Every Style

The Solo Mission: Paddle along the coastal line, discover hidden views, find a quiet moment on the water. A meditative, unforgettable experience.

The Social Paddle: A fantastic group activity — join a guided coastal tour or link up with the local paddling community.

The Family Outing: Stand-up paddleboarding is enjoyable for all ages and fitness levels, creating memories that last a lifetime.

SUP Rental Rates and Booking

Hourly and daily SUP rental rates are available. Boards can be rented for independent exploration or combined with a guided session. The price for an hour on the water is surprisingly accessible, making it one of the best-value water experiences on the island. Contact us via WhatsApp to check availability and current rates.
