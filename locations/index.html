Quick Reference

Which Spot Works Today?

Check the wind direction first — it determines everything on this island.

SE/E
Feb–Apr · Peak
→ Thong Sala
Side-onshore SE/E. Flat-water lagoon. All skill levels. Our school.
→ Baan Tai
Works in SE too. Slightly more chop, good for intermediates.
SW
May–Sep · SW Season
→ Thong Sala
Works in SW. Choppier than in SE season, but still rideable. All levels.
→ Baan Tai
True side-shore SW. Cleanest angle on the island. Intermediate+.
→ Hin Kong
Low-tide only. Experienced riders only — check conditions first.
NE/N
Dec–Jan · NE Season
→ Chaloklum
North shore. NE/N cross-shore. Best NE spot on the island. Advanced only.
Note: 25 min by scooter. Check forecast before the trip.

How to Get Here

✈️
From Bangkok
Fly Bangkok → Koh Samui (1h) or Bangkok → Surat Thani (1h), then ferry to Koh Phangan (30–60 min). Fastest route: Bangkok–Samui–ferry pier, total ~3h.
⛴️
From Koh Samui
Lomprayah or Seatran ferry from Nathon Pier, Koh Samui → Thong Sala Pier, Koh Phangan. Journey time: 30–45 min. Multiple daily sailings. Our school is 5 min walk from the pier.
🏖️
From the Pier
Kite Club is 5 minutes on foot from Thong Sala pier. Walk south along the beach road past the market — look for our sign on the beachfront. GPS: 9.9320, 100.0647.
